Registration Form

Register here for Grace Notes certificate courses. You can register for more than one course. When you register for a course, I will send your first lesson. Each lesson has complete instructions for study and for taking the Quiz. When you return a Quiz to Grace Notes, I will send you the next lesson in your course.

There are no deadlines or schedules. You can study at your own pace. However, I suggest that you complete at least one lesson per week, in order to stay current.

Warren Doud
[To: Course Descriptions]

Please follow the instructions below to register:

1.  Choose the Course you would like to receive:

Register for ONE or TWO courses only. When you have completed the first lesson of any course, you may enroll for another course.

You may register for any course in any unit; however, numbered courses must be completed in succession (e.g., you must complete Doctrine I before you can register for Doctrine II).

UNIT I: Doctrine I  |  Foundations I |  History I |  Ruth  | Titus  

UNIT II: Doctrine II | Ephesians | Foundations II  | Hosea |  Gospel of Mark

UNIT III: Romans |  Doctrine III | History II |  1,2,3 John, Jude, Philemon |  Gospel of Luke

2.  Delivery method:

You will receive each lesson as a Microsoft Word file, attached to an email message. The file is compatible with Windows and Macintosh versions of Word. You will also receive a second email message which contains only the quiz. Each lesson contains the instructions for returning the quiz.

3.  Provide the following information:

Your Name
Email Address (Required: check it for accuracy!)
Address (address, etc., required for mailing certificates)
Address (second line for address, if needed)
City
State (or Province)
Postal Code (Zip)
Country (if not USA)

Your Comment

4. Click ONCE on the Submit button to send your registration to Grace Notes.
